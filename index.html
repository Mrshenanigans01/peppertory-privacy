Privacy Policy — Peppertory

Last updated: 8 July 2026

Peppertory ("the app", "we", "us") is a personal logbook that lets you record and organize information you choose to enter about injections, schedules, inventory, weight, journal notes and biomarker results. This policy explains what the app does with your information.

1. Information we handle

  • Account information (only if you sign in): when you choose to enable optional cloud backup by signing in with Google, we receive your name, email address and a Google account identifier from Google Sign-In.
  • Data you enter: injection logs, peptide/compound names and doses, protocols and schedules, inventory, body weight, journal/symptom notes, and any blood-biomarker links or values you add. Some of this is health information.

The app does not contain advertising, and does not use analytics or tracking SDKs.

2. How your information is stored

  • Without signing in: all data stays on your device only (local storage). We do not receive it.
  • With cloud backup (signed in): your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) under your account so it can sync across your devices. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

3. How we use it

Solely to provide the app's features: to display, calculate, chart and back up the information you enter. We do not sell your data, share it with third parties for advertising, or use it for any purpose other than operating the app for you.

4. Service providers

We use Google Firebase (Authentication and Firestore) as a processor to provide sign-in and cloud storage. Your use of Google Sign-In is also subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Deleting your data

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time inside the app: Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account & All Data. This removes your local data and, if you used cloud backup, your data in Firestore. You may also request deletion by emailing us at the address below.

6. Children

Peppertory is intended only for adults aged 18 or older and is not directed to children.

7. Not medical advice

Peppertory is an informational logbook. It does not provide medical advice and does not diagnose, treat, prescribe, recommend, sell or supply any substance. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making health decisions.

8. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
